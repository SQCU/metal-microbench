## ⚠ BLOCKING FINDING (the adversarial pass) — re-prefill is NOT bit-exact

The prefill K/V projection is qLen/tile-reduction-order dependent (lm_engine.swift:2958, 3600-3602; the .primed recover path is warn-only *because* it is KL-benign, not bit-exact). Re-prefilling an evicted range ULP-drifts K/V and can flip a temp=0 token → violates the sacred bit-exactness invariant. THEREFORE the "reclaim a live generation's pages + re-prefill them" design (B2/B3 core) cannot be bit-exact without reproducing the exact MAX_Q_LEN=256 tile geometry. **Decision (operator value: bit-exactness is sacred, no KL-benign drift): do NOT pause-and-re-prefill live generations.** Instead: reclaim .done pages under pressure (no re-prefill; prefix already in the trie) + shed WHOLE no-consumer generations under page pressure (admission-pressure cancel = kill+free, not pause); live working sets are protected + bounded by admission. This dissolves the leak (continue-on-disconnect zombies generating to max-tokens) without the impossible bit-exact re-prefill.
